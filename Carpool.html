Carpool Connect: Building a Better Carpool System

1.0 Project Overview

Design of an app that help Users search for or create their own carpooling groups.

1.1 Problem

In many urban and suburban areas, people face significant challenges in their daily commutes due to the absence of convenient and accessible carpooling options. Without a reliable platform to facilitate shared rides, individuals often resort to personal vehicles, leading to increased expenses, longer travel times, and negative environmental impacts.

1.2 Solution

To address the challenges faced by commuters due to the lack of carpooling options, Carpool Connect offers a user-friendly platform that enables users to search for or create their own carpooling groups. The app is designed to promote cost-effective, eco-friendly, and socially connected commuting experiences.

2.0 User Centered Design Process

To ensure the solution truly resonated with real user needs, I adopted a user-centered design process throughout the entire development of the Carpool Connect case study — from research and ideation to prototyping and testing.
